Simple Sandwich

Let's make a simple and yummy sandwitch, one of my favourites.

Ingridients :

  • Boneless chicken breast
  • Barbecue sauce
  • Buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion slices

Instructions :

  1. Put the chicken and bbq sauce in the slow cooker.
  2. Cook on high for 5-6 hours or low for 7-8 hours.
  3. Shred meat with a fork.
  4. Toast buns.
  5. Place meat on buns. Top with tomato, onion slices and cheese.
  6. Broil until cheese melts.

Enjoy...
